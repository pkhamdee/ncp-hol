# Application: Building a Chatbot and RAG pipeline

**Objective:**  

This chapter equips you with foundational skills for working with AI application connecting to Nutanix Enterprise AI.

**What You’ll Do:**

1.  **Create Your First Chatbot**
    
    -   Create your first chatbot with Flowise, an open source, low-code tool that enables developers to build customized LLM orchestration flows and AI agents on top of LangChain and/or LlamaIndex. Langchain and LlamaIndex are frameworks for simplifying development of an AI applications.

2.  **Create a RAG pipeline**
    
    -   Enhance your chatbot with Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG), which enables your chatbot to generate answers based on your stored documents.
